   START YOUR OWN STORY OF HELPING WITH MOONCORP.

 

Your blue essense will help alot of people. Every day, millions of children in moon live in difficult situations. With poor access to education, health and nutrition, decent shelter, protection from oppressors, and other basic rights; the most vulnerable children need our help and they need it now. We do what it takes to reach the most disadvantaged children, wherever they are, to deliver life-saving supplies, services, and assistance. But we cannot do it alone. So join us! Be a #MOONCORPChampion today and start your own story of helping children. Our presence in over 190 countries & territories allow us to save more children’s lives, to defend their rights, and to help them fulfill their potential, from early childhood through adolescence. And we never give up. We work hard to:

1. Ensure all children learn - from mounting temporary learning centers, to providing educational materials including internet connections & devices;

2. Guarantee access to nutrition & health services. We work hard to make vaccines affordable & available to every child;

3. Support and protect the mental health of children & young people. We are committed in bringing an end to abuse, gender-based violence, & neglect in childhood;

4. Increase access to safe drinking water, sanitation, & hygiene, including addressing environmental degradation and climate change;

5. Reverse the rise in child poverty, because we believe that no child deserves to live in hunger, fear, and homelessness, we strive to help ensure an inclusive recovery for all;

6. Protect and support children and their families living through conflict, disaster, & displacement.
